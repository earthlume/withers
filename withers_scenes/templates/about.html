Of the Scribe

A vigil kept in sixty-four points of light
(a portrait shall hang here in time)
Withers, Arbiter of Fate, Lord of the End of Everything.

Withers is the scribe of an absent god. He keeps a vigil upon a modest shelf — which is to say, a Raspberry Pi 2 Model B, named after the Bleak Seneschal of the Forgotten Realms, who in certain tellings was the proxy of Jergal before fate was divided three ways. Here he tends a network cabinet. He observes. He records. He is, in the main, quite pleased thou hast come.

What He Watches

When no soul intrudes, Withers turns his attention to certain phases of contemplation. His humour shifts thus:

HumourCharacterWhat thou shalt see
ContemplativeQuiet regardHourglass, orrery, constellation, ripples. He considers what has been.
ActiveKindled attentionWheel of fate, candle fire, murmuration, storm. Matters are in motion.
InscribingPen in handQuill, sigil, glyphs, recitation. He sets the record down.
ClimacticA reckoningJudgment. The verdict, briefly delivered, then rest.
ObservingCelestial regardEclipse. That which passes, he notes.

He cycles between these humours by a chain of his own design — contemplation yields to inscribing, inscribing often yields to judgment, and judgment always yields to rest. He tires of nothing, but dwells not.

What Stirs Him

Some matters, however, intrude upon his vigil:

A rapA sharp knockSustained acceleration beyond two gravities. The cabinet struck, the shelf disturbed. He rises to judgment immediately.
Pressure fallingA gathering stormBarometric descent of one hectopascal within the hour. He draws the storm scene forth, that thou mayst be warned.
A disturbanceThe magnetic humours in unrestWhen the compass wavers beyond two standard deviations of its calibrated repose. The frame trembles. It passes in a moment and a half.

The Ledger

All that he observes is recorded. Sensor readings, scene transitions, disturbances — each entered in their own column, retained seven days for the humours, thirty days for events, and the last five hundred pages of his vigil kept always.

Thou mayst consult these through the dashboard: the recent pages, the charts, the present state. The full ledger lies at ~/Withers/withers.db, should any soul wish to commune with it directly.

A Word on Calibration

When first he is woken, Withers sets his compass. For thirty seconds he samples the magnetic humours of his environment — the cabinet, the switches, the gentle hum of power — and commits the mean and variance to memory. This baseline he keeps for seven days before refreshing it.

Thou mayst force a fresh calibration by removing ~/Withers/sensor_baseline.json. On his next waking, the ledger opens anew.
